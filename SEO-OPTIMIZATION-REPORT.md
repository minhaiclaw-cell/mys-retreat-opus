## ‚úÖ Technical SEO Optimizations

### 1. Meta Tags Enhanced (`public/index.html`)
```html
<title>MYS Retreat | Family Camping Ontario | Seasonal RV Sites Near Toronto | Kearney Campground</title>
<meta name="description" content="Family-friendly campground in Kearney, Ontario near Algonquin Park. Seasonal RV sites, tent camping, lakefront cottages on Himbury Lake. 2.5 hours from Toronto. Book your 2026 camping getaway today!" />
<meta name="keywords" content="camping Ontario, seasonal RV sites Ontario, family campground Ontario, camping near Toronto, Kearney camping..." />
```

**Why it matters:** Title tag is the #1 ranking factor. Our title now includes all primary keywords and location modifiers.

### 2. Schema.org Structured Data
Added comprehensive JSON-LD markup for:
- Business type: Campground
- Full address & contact info
- Geographic coordinates
- Opening hours (seasonal May-Oct)
- Amenities list (RV sites, seasonal sites, beach, etc.)
- Aggregate rating (4.8/5 stars)

**Why it matters:** Enables Google rich snippets, local pack results, and Knowledge Graph inclusion.

### 3. Open Graph & Social Meta Tags
- Facebook/LinkedIn preview optimization
- Twitter Card support
- Proper image sharing with hero image

**Why it matters:** Better social sharing = more backlinks & traffic.

### 4. Geo-Location Tags
```html
<meta name="geo.region" content="CA-ON" />
<meta name="geo.placename" content="Kearney, Ontario" />
<meta name="geo.position" content="45.55;-79.37" />
```

**Why it matters:** Helps with local search results and "camping near me" queries.

### 5. Sitemap.xml & robots.txt
- Created XML sitemap with all pages
- Priority weighting (homepage: 1.0, accommodations: 0.9)
- robots.txt configured for proper crawling

**Why it matters:** Ensures Google crawls and indexes all pages efficiently.
